About us

From the Principal's Desk


Dear Parents & Prospective Students,

Welcoming you to the JDS fold is like welcoming you to a World of educational freedom where knowledge is imparted, attractively wrapped, in multiple folds of values, culture, tradition and ethics, yet giving ample space for an individual's personal growth, uninhibited thought and independent expression.. Here at JDS INTERNATIONAL too we have maintained the tradition of yesteryears. Our students garner major selections in all competitions. Every year we have many such names decorating the school firmament.But the most remarkable aspect of our students is their humaneness. We are proud of our efforts to inculcate in our students not just a will to excel in academics but also to make them mentally inquisitive and emotionally balanced. Today is the time for change, of having the courage to shun the wrong and not only to appreciate and accept the right but to also stand up for it BOLD and STRONG. We at JDS try to instill this courage into our students. We aim to make heroes out of ordinary individuals who have strength to persevere and endure in spite of overwhelming obstacles.Our aim has always been to not just impart knowledge but to give them wisdom. The former to make a living and the latter to make a life. Our overall objective has always been to give to the society better people, true Aryans, who strive in their endeavors to make the world a better place and the universe more noble - I am confident we are achieving it time & time again. Welcome to the world of JDS.....
