OUR MISSION


To promote personal, social and physical development of each student by inculcating values, and developing self confidence, communication skills and thinking skills so that they succeed in a changing world.

Move Forward With Your Education

JDS INTERNATIONAL WE BUILD IN OUR STUDENTS A BELIEF IN SELF AND GOODNESS OF HUMANITY JDS international School, DEORIA works on the philosophy of providing holistic education to all it's pupils while empowering every child with the skills to face the challenges of life. In our mission to provide world class education, we have at our core the concept "Each child matters". The school is based on the faith that each child is born different and this difference needs to be celebrated and nurtured. Every child must be given an opportunity to explore, experience and in turn enrich herself. Books must not restrict her learning nor school limit her ability to dream. Whatever a child learns must be learnt through analysis and application so that she remembers the lessons learnt at school for a whole lifetime. Education must become a joy for life rather than mere means to a career.
